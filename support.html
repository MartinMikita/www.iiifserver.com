Support

IIIFServer is actively developed and updated on regular basis (several releases per year). Customer with active maintenance receive email notification for new releases, with a list of fixed security bugs, information about updated software libraries and new features.

New features can be developed on request and released under open-source license. We can act as partners on European or national research projects.
