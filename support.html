Support

Assistance with installation, configuration, and performance tuning

IIIFServer is actively developed and updated on a regular basis (several releases per year). The customers with active maintenance receive email notification about new releases, with information about the updated functions, new features and fixed security issues.

New features can be developed on request and released under an open-source license. We can act as partners on European or national research projects.
